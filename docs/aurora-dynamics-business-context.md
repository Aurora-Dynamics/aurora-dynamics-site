# Aurora Dynamics — Business Context

**Owner:** Ben
**Industry:** Biotech
**ICP:** CCOs and commercial leads at mid-size biotech companies (where one leader typically owns both sales and marketing)

---

## Core Thesis

The sales and marketing gap in biotech exists not because of poor communication, but because the two functions don't collaborate on shared projects. More meetings don't fix this. Working together on the same deliverables does.

## Core Products (in funnel order)

1. **LinkedIn content & training** — Ghostwriting for executives + training for BD/sales teams. Sales teams should act as marketing satellites, producing content with marketing support rather than marketing owning the company page alone.
2. **Educational email courses** — 5-email opt-in sequences for lead capture. Can be owned by marketing or sales. Works as a KPI for conferences and LinkedIn activity. Sales feeds leads in, monitors activity, and follows up.
3. **Email nurture sequences** — Ongoing nurture emails combining category value content with commercial offers. Sales contributes to messaging and monitors responses. Keeps leads warm until ready to buy.

Each product crosses the sales-marketing boundary and works best as a shared project between both teams.

## Newsletter

**Name:** The Life Science Lead Gap
**Focus:** How life science companies lose leads between sales and marketing — and how to fix it.
**Editorial rule:** Every issue should touch content, lead capture, or lead nurture as the lens for exploring the gap.

## Conference Lead Generation Package (formalising)

A system combining booth design (via designer), pre-event LinkedIn strategy, lead capture at the event, and post-event email nurture. Conferences are a natural collaboration point between sales and marketing. The designer's role sits here — not as a standalone design service.

## Key Strategic Principles

- Products are practical and flexible — can be sold to sales, marketing, or a CCO who owns both
- Positioning is built on strategy and outcomes, not production method
- AI usage in writing workflow is operational, not branded
- Clear beats clever in all communication
- Sequence over ambition: don't build everything at once
