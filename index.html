Our approach

Built to be shared, not siloed

Most marketing deliverables live on one side of the fence. A blog post is "marketing's thing." A sales deck is "the sales team's thing." Neither team touches the other's work, so neither team benefits from the other's perspective.

We design every project to be owned and used by both teams. LinkedIn content trains sales to think like marketers. Email courses give marketing a tool that sales actually uses. Nurture sequences keep leads warm with input from both sides.

The result: fewer leads lost in the gap between first touch and closed deal.
